Web Tip of the Month

Why Mobile Optimization Matters

Did you know over 60% of web traffic comes from mobile devices? A mobile-friendly website is no longer optional. Here are key elements to check:

  • Text is readable without zooming
  • Buttons are large enough to tap
  • Page loads quickly on mobile data
  • No horizontal scrolling needed
